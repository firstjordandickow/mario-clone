Jell-io had done his task,
The Candy Cane, held in his grasp.
But far from done, sure was his quest,
As two more Canes, those were the rest.

"Youv'e done well, o brave squire,"
The King of Treats, said most admire'd.
"Now go down the tower's depths,
The next Cane rests on the farthest step."
